About Me

I am a beginner web developer with a strong passion for learning and creating modern, user-friendly websites. I am currently building my foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy turning ideas and designs into real, functional web pages.

I focus on writing clean, organized code and creating layouts that are responsive and easy to use on all devices. I enjoy learning how websites work behind the scenes and improving my problem-solving skills through hands-on practice and small projects.

I am highly motivated to continue growing as a developer, learning new tools and technologies, and challenging myself with real-world projects. My goal is to become a skilled web developer who builds websites that are both visually appealing and practical.
